My hobbies apart from programming include painting, mathematics, physics, biology, boxing, wrestling, football, and more. I also play Minecraft and Geometry Dash!

    My background

    I am a high school student from India, and was born in Bihar. I started learning how to program pretty early on in around late 2016 to early 2017. I started my journey by learning Java, which is a move I still regret to this day, along with C. I primarily learnt programming through books that my mother/elder cousin(s) had let me borrow, later using online resources to learn Kotlin, Lua, etc.
